Hi-Ho Cherry-O Rules

  1. Each player chooses a tree and fills it with 10 cherries.
  2. Players take turns spinning the spinner and following the instructions based on the spinner result.
  3. If the spinner lands on a number, the player removes that many cherries from their tree and puts them in the bucket.
  4. If the spinner lands on the bird or dog, the player returns 2 cherries from their bucket to their tree.
  5. If the spinner lands on the spilled bucket, the player returns all cherries from their bucket to their tree.
  6. The first player to move all 10 cherries from their tree to their bucket wins the game.
